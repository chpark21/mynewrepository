How to Make Instant Cup Noodles

Hey there! Have you never made an instant noodle before? Well, let's get started!

  1. Tear the lid of the Cup Noodles halfway.
  2. Take out all the seasoning packets inside.
  3. Carefully tear the plastic packet with powdered seasoning, and pour it onto the noodle.
  4. Add boiling water into the cup until it reaches the line inside. If for some reason you cannot find the line, pour the boiling water for around 2/3 of the cup.
  5. Reseal the lid and put a book(or something to cover the lid)
  6. Wait for around 2 to 3 minutes and get your chopstick.
  7. Enjoy!
